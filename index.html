2. Five Patient Communication Concepts for McLaren

A modernized single patient front door.

Improve routine patient communications into verified, secure, branded, interactive conversations.

Verified care journey thread

Replace disconnected email and SMS reminders with one branded McLaren conversation that stays with the patient from referral to follow-up.

  • Verified sender identity to reduce confusion and phishing anxiety.
  • Rich cards for location, provider, preparation steps, forms, and next actions.
  • Fallback to SMS or email when RCS is unavailable, preserving continuity.
